About me

I'm Suzanne Boon, a 16-year-old student on the path to becoming a Software Developer. Here I will share some information about myself and why I want to become a Software Developer.
My intrest for creating things began when I was just a child. I always wanted to start new projects of any kind and make something from my own hands. I liked being creative and decorating anything and everything. Since that time I got more intrest in the digital world instead, using it everyday like a lot of us. Online I played a lot of games that big companies made or indie developers. I was facinated and insipred at what some people could do, and I knew this was something I wanted to do too. After that, I started my journey of becoming a Software Developer in September 2023 by joining the course at MBO Rijnland.
I am excited to share my progress in this journey of becoming a Software Developer with you.

The application supports saving and loading noted to and from a local file, provinding a convenient way to store and retrieve your written text.
With a simple and clean interface, this application offers a user-friendly platform for managing and customizing your digital notes.
