About me

I'm Suzanne Boon, a 16-year-old student on the path to becoming a Software Developer. Here I will share some information about myself and why I want to become a Software Developer.
My intrest for creating things began when I was just a child. I always wanted to start new projects of any kind and make something from my own hands. I liked being creative and decorating anything and everything. Since that time I got more intrest in the digital world instead, using it everyday like a lot of us. Online I played a lot of games that big companies made or indie developers. I was facinated and insipred at what some people could do, and I knew this was something I wanted to do too. After thinking about it long enough, i started my journey of becoming a Software Developer in August 2023 by joining the course at MBO Rijnland. I am excited to share my progress in this journey of becoming a Software Developer with you.
